## 🚀 Our Mission

Founded in **2025**. Genrobot — Embodied Intelligence Model delivers high-volume, diverse, multi-modal and high-precision data solutions. Human-centric — comprehensively captures the modal interactions between humans and the world, covering head, hand and full-body movements. Spanning multiple scenarios including household, manufacturing and logistics.
